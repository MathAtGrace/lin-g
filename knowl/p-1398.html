For the following vectors,

  1. \(\bvec\) is in \(\laspan{\vvec_1,\vvec_2,\vvec_3}\text{.}\)

  2. \(\vvec_3\) is in \(\laspan{\vvec_1,\vvec_2,\vvec_3}\text{.}\)

  3. \(\bvec\) is not in \(\laspan{\vvec_1,\vvec_2,\vvec_3}\text{.}\)

  4. \(\laspan{\vvec_1,\vvec_2,\vvec_3}\) is a plane in \(\real^3\text{.}\)
